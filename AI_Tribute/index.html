Max Verstappen

Formula 1 WORLD CHAMPION 2022

Max Emilian Verstappen (born 30 September 1997) is a Belgian-Dutch[2] racing driver and the 2021 Formula One World Champion.
He competes under the Dutch flag in Formula One with Red Bull Racing. He is the son of former Formula One driver Jos Verstappen.

As of the 2022 Italian Grand Prix, Verstappen has achieved 31 victories and 17 pole positions. He scored the first hat-trick of his career at the 2021 French Grand Prix and his first grand slam at the 2021 Austrian Grand Prix.
He scored his second grand slam at the 2022 Emilia Romagna Grand Prix. Verstappen is set to remain at Red Bull until at least the end of the 2028 season after signing a contract extension.
